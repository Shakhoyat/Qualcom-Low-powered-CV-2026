# 2026 LPCVC Track 1 Overview

Source: https://lpcv.ai/2026LPCVC/tracks/track1/

## Task
- Open-World Image-to-Text Retrieval.

## Key Timeline
- Submission window: March 1, 2026 to April 30, 2026 (ET).

## Platform
- Hardware: Qualcomm XR2 Gen 2 (Proxy on AI Hub).
- Software: Qualcomm AI Hub.

## Prerequisites
- Team registration on LPCVC portal.
- Qualcomm AI Hub account for each member as needed.

## Submission Flow
- Compile and validate model on Qualcomm AI Hub.
- Share compile job/model access with lowpowervision@gmail.com.
- Fill official Track 1 submission form with compile job ID.
- A submission is not evaluated unless both sharing and form submission are completed.

## Evaluation Summary
- Stage 1: latency validity threshold.
- Stage 2: quality metric for valid submissions (Recall@Top10).
- Leaderboard updated periodically by organizers.

## Notes
- Use the official sample solution for expected input/output format.
- Keep your own local validation; do not rely only on leaderboard cycles.
