# 2026 LPCVC Introduction Notes

Source: https://lpcv.ai/2026LPCVC/introduction/

## Competition Scope
- 2026 Low-Power Computer Vision Challenge focuses on practical edge CV workloads.
- Emphasis on efficient deployment on Qualcomm-supported devices through AI Hub.
- Accepts common training/export formats including PyTorch, TensorFlow, TFLite, and ONNX.

## High-Level Participation Flow
1. Register team and complete required agreement.
2. Create Qualcomm AI Hub accounts.
3. Build and validate per-track model pipelines.
4. Submit according to track instructions during submission window.

## Operational Notes
- Public leaderboard updates are based on organizer evaluation cadence.
- Winning solutions are expected to be open-sourced per challenge policy.
- Use newsletter and Qualcomm AI Hub Slack for official updates.

## Team Reminder
- Treat track pages as source of truth for final submission requirements.
- Keep experiment records including model version, compile job ID, and submission timestamp.
